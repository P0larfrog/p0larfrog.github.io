STEM

STEM is all about exploring the exciting worlds of Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics! Here, students dive into hands-on projects, tackle real-world problems, and develop critical thinking skills. This program not only prepares you for college but also opens doors to rewarding careers in fields like engineering, healthcare, and tech. Join us to unleash your imagination and get ready to become a master of innovation, ready to tackle challenges with fun!

HUMSS

HUMSS explore the exciting worlds of Humanities and Social Sciences, where students explore human behavior, culture, and society. Through engaging subjects like literature, politics, and the arts, they develop critical thinking and communication skills. This strand prepares them for college and careers in fields like education, psychology, law, and journalism. Join us to uncover the stories that shape our world and become a voice for change!

GAS

Ready to explore endless possibilities? The General Academic Strand (GAS) offers a well-rounded education that opens doors to various career paths! Prepare for college and discover your passions with us in the General Academic Strand!

TVL

The Technical-Vocational Livelihood (TVL) Track at our school features two exciting strands. Whether you want to code the next big app or work with electrical systems, an adventure awaits. Let’s discover your passion together!

More

TVL - ICT

Dive into the exciting world of technology with the ICT strand! If you love gadgets, coding, and creativity, this is the perfect place for you— with TESDA certifications under your belt, you'll be ready to launch your career in the digital universe right after graduation. Get ready to innovate and inspire!

TVL - EIM

Are you ready to spark your future? The EIM strand is all about turning your passion for electricity into a thrilling career! Learn to install and maintain electrical systems while gaining practical skills through exciting projects. With TESDA certifications, you’ll be all set for a bright career in electrical engineering. Power up your potential with us!
